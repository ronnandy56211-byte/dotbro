The Context
Mandi is not just
a campus town.
A landslide-prone, remotely connected, underserved hill district — where daily gaps become crises fast.
🏔️
Landslide-prone region
Road cuts, supply chain breaks, isolated communities during monsoon. Trusted local networks matter more than apps.
📦
E-commerce dead zone
Amazon & Flipkart reach the highway. The last mile — from town to IIT Kamand and village homes — is completely broken.
🏫
Campus cut off
IIT Mandi at Kamand is 15km from town. Students wait days for deliveries that could take hours with the right network.
.BRO exists to stitch these gaps — using trusted local people, not platforms.

Started with a ₹500 Himshikhar project. The problem was too real to leave as a weekend experiment.

I saw students hungry with no options. I saw local boys in Mandi looking for work. I saw vendors with great food and no reach. Landslide roads cutting off communities.

.BRO is the answer — not one product, but a network that stitches the gaps wherever they appear.
